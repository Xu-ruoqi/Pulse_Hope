Rescue And Adoption

Pulse Hope is a pet rescue and adoption platform with life care at its core. We believe that every life deserves to be treated gently. The platform name "Mai Wang" comes from the meaning of "extending the pulse of life, watching for the future", which means using "Pulse" to connect the emotional resonance between people and animals, lighting the light of hope in difficulties with "Hope", and building a warm bridge of life for stray animals and caring people.

Adoption Guidelines

* Adoption is pro bono, that is, no adoption fee will be charged. Adopters also do not have to pay for emergency medical expenses, food, transportation expenses and other expenses incurred after the pet is left behind by the original owner. Dear adopters can help with the daily expenses of the shelter through a single donation or monthly sponsorship plan. Thank you!

1

Pick the right "kids" to be your partner

Keep an eye out for live updates on our instagram feed, or contact us to make an appointment to visit the animals in person at the shelter. We hope that you can visit and interact with the animals several times, and confirm your willingness before applying.
Reservation call: +852-4479633

2

Send an application

This platform does not support the application of others, interested adopters should apply in person, immediately fill in the "application form". Please prepare a video in advance of the applicant's visit. The video should briefly introduce the applicant's :

  • Home environment and address
  • Indoor and balcony spaces
  • Pet rest and toilet areas
3

Wait for the approval of the platform to become a "qualified applicant"

After the applicant submits the application form, the platform will conduct matching assessment, such as: the applicant's financial ability, the number of residents, the number of existing pets, the time they can spend with their pets, the breed and personality of the pets to be adopted. The assessment will be completed within 10 working days after the application is submitted. The approved adopter will receive a call from our trainer or tutor to arrange a time to check the adoption with us.

4

Submit the documents required for adoption

If the applicant chooses to support a pet or make a donation, he/she must complete the sponsoring or donation procedure on the same day.

  • Copy of the adopter's 「ID card」
  • The 「proof of residential address」 document in the adopter's name
5

Receive training and sign an agreement

The shelter instructor will instruct the owners about pet rearing information, such as diet size and cycle, the date of the next parasite prevention, the date of rabies injection, whether spaying or neutering is necessary, etc.
After the completion of the training, the formal handover is carried out, and the adopter signs the adoption agreement on the same day to complete the adoption.

About Us

Pulse Hope is a non-profit pet rescue platform made up of volunteers, with a professional animal medical rescue team, pet trainers and pet health managers.

The purpose of the platform is to spread the right care for animals and ensure that all small animals that receive treatment and shelter from the platform are cared for until a suitable adoptive family is found.

Our platform's services include :

Emergency Rescue:

At any time

Rescue care for sick, injured and abused pets;
Pet medicine;
Emergency operation;
Reasonable placement;
Socialization training;
Adoption without compensation;
Foster care.

Free pet grooming:

Reservation required

Enhance skills for unemployed people;
Provide extracurricular activities for students;
Provide pet grooming knowledge and practice.

Volunteer Activities:

Twice a month

Co-organize animal volunteer activities with different youth groups;
Set up a volunteer award scheme to encourage young people to develop good moral character and love animals.
